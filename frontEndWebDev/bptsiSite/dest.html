Machu Picchu

Located in the Peruvian Andes, the ancient Inck city of Machu Picchu was constructed in the 1400s at the height of the Inca Empire.

It was abandoned less than 100 years later when the Inca empire collapsed under Spanish conquest. Today Machu Picchu is the most visited tourist attraction in Peru.

An inspiring poem about Machu Picchu

Macchu Picchu

Spanish:

from the side of the mountain
as God's ladder,
memories of a time gone by
of Inca glory and pride.
In the middle of the mountains,
Machu Picchu, city of stone,
sing a song
of Inca glory and pride.
I'm looking forward to going back
To a place where I haven't been.
to have memories
of Inca glory and pride.

Spanish:


Lying on the mountain side
like stairs of the gods,
memories of a distant past
of glory and Inca pride.
Among the mountain peaks,
Machu Picchu, a city of stone,
sing a song
of glory and Inca pride.
I want to return to
a place I've never been.
I want to have memories
of glory and Inca pride.


© Jim T. Henriksen
September 16th, 2005
